# Purchase recommendation from captured evidence

## Verified requirement

For the specific Aspire 3 A325-45 discussed in the issue, the strongest mutually consistent evidence is:

- laptop label in the captured dialog: 12 V DC, 2 A;
- official Acer indexed specification: 24 W power supply;
- 12 V × 2 A = 24 W.

The connector is likely 3.5 × 1.35 mm and center-positive based on the model-specific marketplace evidence and the photographed jack analysis in the supplied dialog, but this study did not obtain an Acer service manual or live authoritative connector specification. Measure the existing plug or obtain seller confirmation before purchase.

Never substitute a 19 V Acer adapter merely because its plug fits: voltage must match exactly. A supply rated above 2 A is acceptable only when voltage, connector dimensions, and polarity match; the laptop draws the current it needs.

## Amazon India result

The strongest Amazon India candidate found by the project's own search adapter is [Tonton ASIN B0CG66QYWN](https://www.amazon.in/Tonton-Adapter-Charger-Interchangeable-3-5x1-35/dp/B0CG66QYWN). Its indexed Amazon title states 12 V, 2 A, and 24 W; the listing URL names an interchangeable 3.5 × 1.35 mm tip. Those values match the electrical requirement and likely connector size without adding a second inline adapter.

This remains a **candidate, not a confirmed match**. Amazon returned its automated-access page in the real Chromium capture, the indexed result does not name A325-45 or state polarity, and current stock was not visible. Before ordering, confirm that the included 3.5 × 1.35 mm tip is center-positive, the output can be fixed at 12 V, the item is in stock, and it is returnable. Also measure the original plug if possible.

No direct Amazon India listing was verified live to satisfy all four conditions—12 V, at least 2 A, 3.5 × 1.35 mm, center-positive—during the 2026-07-19 capture. Therefore neither the Tonton candidate nor either fallback should be labelled guaranteed compatible.

Prefer the one-piece Tonton candidate over the two-piece fallback if the seller confirms its polarity and tip.
